Our Partner Universities

Universidad Politécnica de Madrid

Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM) is the oldest and largest technical university in Spain, offering a wide range of engineering, architecture, and computer science programs. UPM has a strong international presence, participating in networks like Magalhães and EELISA, and engages in numerous international student exchange programs.

Visit UPM Website

Belgrade Metropolitan University

Belgrade Metropolitan University (BMU) is a modern institution with diverse bachelor’s, master’s, and PhD programs, along with lifelong learning opportunities. BMU emphasizes e-learning and innovative teaching methods, with active research centers in e-learning and software development, contributing to projects in personalized and online learning.

Visit BMU Website

University of Eastern Finland

The University of Eastern Finland (UEF) is known for addressing global challenges through multidisciplinary research in fields like aging, health, and environmental sustainability. With two campuses, UEF offers extensive international collaborations and research, especially in artificial intelligence, data science, and educational technology.

Visit UEF Website

Universidad Internacional de La Rioja

Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR) is a 100% online university providing high-quality, personalized education. With a global student base, UNIR offers over 400 degrees in both Spanish and English, with a focus on technology-enhanced learning to meet industry needs across Latin America and Europe.

Visit UNIR Website
