Results of the project:

* A methodology to design, and exemplary instructional scenarios of using pedagogically sound educational escape rooms (EERs).

* A virtual platform to create, conduct and share EERs (GitHub link).

* Courses and materials for training teachers in the use of the methodology and the platform.

* A series of EERs, which will be executed and evaluated to validate the methodology and the platform.

* A European network dedicated to the creation, sharing and execution of EERs.

* Multiplier events that will extend the projectâ€™s impact to a broader audience.

* Articles providing a better understanding of EERs.

By empowering educators to create and conduct EERs, we will stimulate the sustainable adoption of innovative learning and teaching practices in the participating institutions and beyond. In addition, through the integration of innovative digital teaching tools in the participating institutions and the training of teachers on how to create and digitize EERs, we address digital transformation through the development of digital readiness, resilience, and capacity.
